## Nov 2024 Monthly Metrics

**HOOD Individual Research Analysis - 001**

**Report**: Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD): Monthly: Stronger equity and crypto volumes, and balance sheet expansion 
**Date**: 11 December 2024 
**Source file**: `G:/gs/RobinhoodMarketsInc.(HOOD)_Monthly_Strongerequityandcryptovolumes,andbalancesheetexpansion.pdf` 
**Deduplication note**: Unique HOOD report in the local batch.

**Conclusion**

This report is a classic monthly KPI-upgrade note. Goldman Sachs remained Buy-rated and raised the 12-month price target to `$48` from `$46`, using a Q5-Q8 P/E multiple of `30.5x`. The report price was `$37.84`, implying `26.8%` upside at that time. The central conclusion is that November 2024 monthly data showed a strong post-election acceleration in Robinhood engagement, especially crypto trading, equity trading, margin balances, AUC and funded accounts. The data supported higher trading revenue, higher NII assumptions, and higher EPS forecasts.

**Key data**

- November funded accounts rose `2% MoM` to `24.8mn`, up about `6% YoY`.
- End-of-period AUC rose `22% MoM` to about `$194.6bn`, equivalent to more than `260%` annualized AUC growth for the month.
- Net cumulative deposits increased about `$5.6bn` sequentially to `$152.9bn`.
- Equity, options, and crypto DARTs rose `25% / 10% / 400%` sequentially to about `2.5mn / 1.0mn / 1.0mn`.
- Equity volume increased `16% MoM` to about `$147bn`; options contract volume was roughly `156mn`, down `2%` sequentially; crypto volume rose about `530%` sequentially to about `$35bn`.
- Margin balances rose to `$6.8bn` from `$6.2bn` in October and surpassed the November 2021 historical peak.
- Cash sweeps increased `4% MoM` to about `$26.5bn`, including `$25.7bn` Gold Cash Sweeps.
- Securities lending revenue rose to about `$23mn` from `$19mn` in the prior month.
- App downloads improved, with YTD average monthly downloads of about `435k`, vs. about `265k` monthly average in 2023.

**Estimate and valuation changes**

Goldman raised 2024E/2025E/2026E transaction-based revenue by `3% / 4% / 3%`, raised 2025E/2026E NII by `1%`, and raised 2025E/2026E total net revenue by `2%`. EPS estimates increased by `3% / 5% / 3%` for 2024E/2025E/2026E. Forecast revenue was `$2.8315bn` in 2024E, `$3.3094bn` in 2025E and `$3.6932bn` in 2026E; forecast EPS was `$1.25`, `$1.58` and `$1.57` for those years.

**Investment interpretation**

The most important message is that HOOD was already shifting from a single-product retail brokerage story into a platform whose revenue sensitivity increases sharply when retail risk appetite improves. November 2024 showed three reinforcing drivers: higher trading activity, higher customer assets, and higher balance-sheet monetization. Crypto was the most explosive driver, but the breadth across equity, options, margin and cash sweep balances made the report stronger than a one-off crypto spike.

The report also shows why HOOD can have high operating leverage. When customer activity rises, transaction revenue, securities lending, margin interest and sweep economics can all improve together. The risk is that the same cyclicality works in reverse if retail engagement fades.

**Investment advice from the report / my reading**

At the report date, the Buy call was supported by clear KPI acceleration and positive estimate revisions. For a long-term investor, this note was a confirmation that HOOD had become more asset-gathering and balance-sheet-sensitive than the market may have appreciated. However, because the catalyst was partly post-election risk appetite and crypto enthusiasm, this was not a low-volatility compounding thesis. It was a high-beta growth platform thesis.

**Risks**

Key downside risks listed by Goldman: lower retail engagement, lower interest rates pressuring NII, weaker cross-sell of new products into the existing user base, lower margins, and payment-for-order-flow regulation. My additional reading risk: November was an unusually strong month, so extrapolating one month into a normalized run-rate would be dangerous unless deposits, funded accounts and trading activity remained durable.

## Tokenized Equities Crypto Staking EU Perps

**HOOD Individual Research Analysis - 002**

**Report**: Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD): New product announcements, including tokenized US equities, crypto staking, and EU perpetual futures 
**Date**: 30 June 2025 
**Primary source file**: `G:/gs/9.Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD)_ New product announcements, incl. tokenized US equities, crypto staking, and EU perpetual futures.pdf` 
**Deduplication note**: Exact duplicate copies also appeared in the root folder and WeChat July 2025 folder. They were merged and summarized once.

**Conclusion**

This report analyzes Robinhood's June 2025 crypto and tokenization product expansion. Goldman remained Buy-rated with a 12-month price target of `$91`, based on `46.5x` Q5-Q8 P/E. The report price was `$93.63`, implying `2.8%` downside to the target at that time, even though the formal rating remained Buy. The important point is not near-term upside in the target price, but the strategic shift: Robinhood was trying to expand from US retail brokerage into a global, crypto-native financial infrastructure platform.

**Key announcements**

- Tokenized US stocks and ETFs for eligible European customers, with zero commissions, a `10bps` FX conversion fee, dividend support and `24/5` trading.
- Tokens are derivative contracts under MiFID II. They provide economic exposure but not ownership or voting rights in the underlying shares.
- Robinhood also tokenized and gifted small quantities of private-company exposure, specifically OpenAI and SpaceX, to European users.
- Robinhood Europe initially records tokenized stock and ETF contracts on Arbitrum, with a plan to migrate to Robinhood's own layer 2 blockchain.
- The planned HOOD layer 2 chain is designed for tokenized real-world assets, `24/7` trading and eventually private equity trading.
- US crypto staking launched for eligible US customers, initially for Ethereum and Solana.
- Until 1 October 2025, customers receive `100%` of staking rewards; afterward, customers receive `75%` and HOOD retains `25%`.
- EU perpetual futures were announced with continuous exposure, up to `3x` leverage, a `2bps` commission on notional value, and routing through Bitstamp.

**Forecast and valuation data**

Goldman forecast revenue of `$2.951bn / $3.7366bn / $4.2733bn / $4.9289bn` for 2024/2025E/2026E/2027E. Forecast EPS was `$2.01 / $1.60 / $1.83 / $2.22`. The implied P/E was `10.9x / 58.7x / 51.1x / 42.2x`. The high forward multiples show that by mid-2025, the stock was already discounting a large amount of future growth.

**Investment interpretation**

The strategic importance is large. Tokenized US equities in Europe allow HOOD to offer US market exposure using crypto licensing infrastructure rather than traditional brokerage licensing. This could lower the friction of international expansion and give Robinhood a differentiated global product. Private equity tokenization, if eventually permitted and liquid, could be more important than public equity tokenization because it gives retail investors access to assets historically reserved for institutions and accredited investors.

Staking and perpetual futures are also important because they move HOOD closer to Coinbase/crypto-exchange economics while staying inside the Robinhood user experience. The Bitstamp route matters because it gives HOOD crypto market infrastructure instead of being only a front-end retail app.

**Investment advice from the report / my reading**

At the report price, the stock was already above Goldman's target, so the immediate risk/reward was not obviously attractive on price target math alone. The reason to stay interested was strategic optionality: tokenized equities, staking, perpetual futures, private market access and a proprietary layer 2 chain could each expand TAM. This is a product-optionality thesis, not a cheap-stock thesis.

**Risks**

Key risks include lower retail engagement, interest-rate sensitivity through NII, weaker cross-sell of new products, lower margins, and payment-for-order-flow regulation. Additional risks from this product set include tokenization regulatory scrutiny, private equity token controversy, execution risk in layer 2 infrastructure, crypto cycle cyclicality, and possible fee compression if competitors replicate staking/perps.

## HOOD Summit Social Trading Short Selling

**HOOD Individual Research Analysis - 003**

**Report**: Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD): HOOD Summit - New product announcements, notably social trading and short selling 
**Date**: 10 September 2025 
**Source file**: `G:/gs/wechat/xwechat_files/piao433402_6a39/msg/file/2025-09/14.Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD)_ HOOD Summit - New product announcements, notably social trading and short selling.pdf` 
**Deduplication note**: Unique HOOD Summit report in the local batch.

**Conclusion**

This report covers Robinhood's second annual HOOD Summit. Goldman stayed Buy-rated and set a 12-month price target of `$130`, based on `54.5x` Q5-Q8 P/E. The report price was `$117.46`, implying `10.7%` upside. The main conclusion is that short selling and Robinhood Social were the two most strategically meaningful product announcements. Short selling improves product parity with advanced brokers; social trading could increase platform engagement and user stickiness beyond transaction activity.

**Key product announcements**

- Short selling expected by the end of 2025, representing a meaningful infrastructure upgrade for advanced trading.
- Robinhood Social: a verified trading community where users can follow real traders, view live verified trades and performance metrics, and trade directly from the social feed.
- Robinhood Social to launch by invitation only in early 2026 at no additional cost.
- Robinhood Legend expanded to include `40+` CME Group futures products, including S&P 500, oil, Bitcoin and gold.
- Futures commissions can be as low as `$0.50` with Gold membership.
- Trading ladder widget added to view L2 and P&L data and make one-click trades.
- Custom indicators and custom scans are planned, helping users do technical analysis and screen opportunities across stocks, ETFs, crypto and futures.
- Overnight index options expected in early 2026.
- Multiple individual brokerage accounts introduced.
- Mobile trading ladder expansion planned for stocks, ETFs and crypto in the US, plus UK stock ladder availability.

**Forecast and valuation data**

Goldman forecast revenue of `$2.951bn / $4.3234bn / $4.9262bn / $5.4530bn` for 2024/2025E/2026E/2027E. EPS forecasts were `$2.01 / $2.09 / $2.27 / $2.55`. Implied P/E was `10.9x / 56.1x / 51.7x / 46.1x`. The valuation was demanding, but Goldman viewed product expansion and platform depth as enough to justify a premium multiple.

**Investment interpretation**

This report is about HOOD becoming a more complete active-trader platform. Historically Robinhood was strongest with younger retail users and simple mobile trading. Short selling, futures, technical tools, trading ladders and overnight index options push the product into more sophisticated trader territory. That matters because advanced traders usually produce higher trading frequency and more revenue per account.

Robinhood Social is different: it is not just another trading tool, but a potential attention and community layer. If successful, it could increase app time, trust, idea generation and copying/following behavior. This can improve retention and make HOOD less dependent on paid marketing. The open question is whether social trading increases quality engagement or creates compliance/reputational risk.

**Investment advice from the report / my reading**

This was a positive strategic report, but with only moderate target-price upside at the time. The strongest investable point is that Robinhood was expanding both vertically and horizontally: more sophisticated products for active users, and more engagement/community features for everyday users. If these products convert into higher DARTs, higher balances and higher Gold penetration, the multiple can remain high. But if they remain announcements without adoption, the valuation leaves little room for disappointment.

**Risks**

Goldman lists lower retail engagement, lower interest rates affecting NII, weaker cross-sell of new products, lower margins, and payment-for-order-flow regulation. My added risks: short selling and social trading may increase regulatory scrutiny, customer-loss complaints, suitability issues and brand risk if users follow poor-performing traders.

The report's title is accurate: the account count wobble matters, but the balance-sheet expansion was more important. HOOD's growth was no longer just about trade count. Platform assets, cash sweeps, margin balances, securities lending and Bitstamp crypto volume all expanded the revenue base. The large YoY increases suggest HOOD had structurally better engagement than a year earlier, even if August cooled from July.

This report also highlights how Robinhood's business mix was becoming more institutional and balance-sheet driven. Bitstamp contributed more than half of reported crypto volume in the month, which means crypto infrastructure and institutional flow were becoming relevant to the company-level KPI picture.

**Investment advice from the report / my reading**

The Buy thesis was still intact, but less clean than in a pure acceleration month. The best interpretation is that HOOD remained a growth platform with strong YoY momentum, but investors needed to watch funded account growth and sequential engagement. At a high multiple, the company cannot rely only on AUC and margin balances; it must keep proving that product launches convert into active, funded, revenue-generating users.

**Risks**

Goldman lists lower retail engagement, lower interest rates affecting NII growth, weaker cross-sell of new products, lower margins, and payment-for-order-flow regulation. My added risk: if account growth stalls while product complexity rises, HOOD may become more dependent on a smaller cohort of active traders, increasing cyclicality.

If that is right, the valuation framework should not be a conventional broker multiple. If wrong, the multiple is too high for a cyclical trading company.

**Investment advice from the report / my reading**

This was a strong bull-case report, but it requires high confidence in execution. The best investor posture is to treat HOOD as a long-duration optionality platform: prediction markets, tokenization, international and institutional/RIA expansion could each become major businesses. But because the target rests on a very high multiple, position sizing should reflect execution risk. The thesis needs evidence in user adoption, revenue mix diversification and regulatory progress.

Additional key risks: prediction market legal/regulatory risk, crypto regulatory delays, tokenization rules, international licensing, TradePMR integration risk, and the chance that the superapp vision becomes too broad and dilutes focus.

## Agentic Trading and Credit Card

**HOOD Individual Research Analysis - 006**

**Report**: Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD): Announcement of agentic trading and credit card 
**Date**: 27 May 2026 
**Source file**: `G:/gs/wechat/xwechat_files/piao433402_6a39/msg/file/2026-06/13.Robinhood Markets Inc. (HOOD)_ Announcement of agentic trading and credit card.pdf` 
**Deduplication note**: Unique 2026 AI/agentic product report in the local HOOD batch.

**Conclusion**

This report analyzes Robinhood's announcement of agentic trading and agentic credit card features. Goldman stayed Buy-rated with a 12-month price target of `$94`, based on `33.0x` Q5-Q8 P/E. The report price was `$74.09`, implying `26.9%` upside. The central conclusion is that this is an early attempt to embed AI agents directly into retail brokerage and consumer finance. Goldman sees potential strategic significance, but emphasizes that TAM and adoption are hard to assess because agentic banking and brokerage products are still new and unproven.

**Key product details**

- Agentic trading announced on 27 May 2026.
- Customers can connect their own AI agents to Robinhood's AI-native Model Context Protocol, or MCP.
- Customers open dedicated agentic trading accounts that are separate from the rest of their portfolio.
- The agent can access only the funds inside the dedicated account, not the customer's entire asset base.
- Customers receive push notifications when agents trade.
- Customers can turn off an agent with one click.
- The beta launch supports equities first.
- HOOD plans to add agentic trading for crypto, event contracts, futures and other products over time.
- Agentic credit card lets agents look for best prices, monitor product availability and purchase automatically.
- The customer connects the agent to a virtual HOOD Gold Card, sets spending limits and chooses whether to require manual approvals.
- The agentic card cannot access the customer's primary HOOD card or other HOOD account information.
- The agentic card initially supports HOOD Gold Card; Platinum Card support is planned after Platinum launches later in 2026.
- Safety controls include spending controls, limited account access, instant shutoff, dispute support and default conservative trading approval settings.

**Forecast and valuation data**

Goldman forecast revenue of `$4.473bn / $5.0473bn / $6.2246bn / $7.2952bn` for 2025/2026E/2027E/2028E. Forecast EPS was `$2.36 / $2.17 / $2.69 / $3.30`. Implied P/E was `37.0x / 34.2x / 27.5x / 22.5x`. The lower target multiple vs. late-2025 management-meeting note shows that valuation expectations had reset, but Goldman still viewed the stock favorably at the report price.

**Investment interpretation**

The significance is that HOOD is trying to own the interface between consumers and financial AI agents. If AI agents become common for shopping, payments, investing and portfolio management, the account infrastructure and permission layer become strategically important. Robinhood's MCP could make HOOD a platform that agents connect to, rather than just an app humans open.

The design choices are important: separate accounts, virtual cards, limits, approvals and one-click shutoff are all meant to reduce the trust barrier. Without those controls, agentic brokerage would be difficult to adopt. The product is early, but the architecture is aimed at solving the central consumer objection: giving an AI agent financial power is scary unless access is limited and reversible.

**Investment advice from the report / my reading**

This is not a near-term earnings report; it is a strategic option. It should not be valued as if agentic finance revenue is already visible. The right way to use it is as evidence that HOOD is moving quickly into AI-native financial infrastructure. If adoption starts to show up in Gold retention, card usage, account engagement, trading frequency or new-product cross-sell, it could become meaningful. Until then, it is an option embedded in the platform.

At the report price of `$74.09`, Goldman's target implied attractive upside. Relative to the latest market price check of about `$93.19` on 13 June 2026, the stock is already close to this specific report's `$94` target, so the report's original upside has largely been realized. Future upside would need either higher estimates, a higher multiple, or successful proof that agentic products create real incremental revenue.

**Risks**

Goldman lists lower retail engagement, lower interest rates affecting NII, weaker cross-sell of new products, lower margins and payment-for-order-flow regulation. Additional risks: consumer trust barriers, AI-agent errors, disputed trades or purchases, cybersecurity risk, suitability and fiduciary questions, and fast imitation by larger banks/brokers if the product proves attractive.

**4. 风险画像**
HOOD风险非常清楚：
- 零售交易热度下降会直接打交易收入、DARTs、crypto量、options量。
- 利率下降会压制NII、cash sweep和margin相关收益。
- PFOF监管、crypto监管、prediction markets监管、tokenization监管都是核心变量。
- AI agent交易/消费如果出纠纷，可能带来合规和声誉风险。
- 竞争者会快速复制成功产品，尤其是staking、agentic card、AI交易助手、tokenized equities。
- 估值本身很脆弱。本地 `drawdown_35_results.csv` 显示HOOD上市以来最大回撤约 **-90.2%**，历史上多次出现35%以上回撤，说明它不是低波动复利股，而是高弹性、高回撤标的。

**投资判断**
如果只按本地最新GS研报，HOOD是 Buy，但目前市场价已接近GS最新目标价 **$94**，短线安全边际不如5月26日报告时好。更合理的策略是：把HOOD放在“高成长高波动金融科技观察池”，等待回调或等待关键KPI确认，而不是无保护追高。
